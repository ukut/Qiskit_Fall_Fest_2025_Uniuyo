ABOUT THE EVENT

Welcome to the Qiskit Fall Fest 2025 at University of Uyo! As we celebrate 100 years of quantum mechanics, this groundbreaking event brings together students, researchers, and quantum computing enthusiasts for an immersive journey into the quantum realm.


Our pre-workshop classes, held every Thursday starting September 25th through October 31st, will provide foundational knowledge and hands-on experience with quantum computing concepts, preparing participants for the main workshop on November 06th-08th, 2025.

100
Years of Quantum Mechanics

From Max Planck's quantum theory in 1900 to today's quantum computers, we celebrate a century of revolutionary discoveries that changed our understanding of reality and opened doors to technologies once thought impossible.

PRE-WORKSHOP SCHEDULE

Week 1
Thursday, September 25th, 2025

Introduction to Quantum Mechanics & Computing Fundamentals

Dive into the fascinating world of quantum mechanics, exploring superposition, entanglement, and the fundamental principles that make quantum computing possible. Learn about the historical journey from classical to quantum computing.

Week 2
Thursday, October 2nd, 2025

Qiskit Framework & Quantum Circuits

Hands-on introduction to IBM Qiskit, building your first quantum circuits, understanding quantum gates, and exploring quantum algorithms. Interactive coding sessions with real quantum simulators and hardware.

Week 3- Week 6
Thursdays, October(09th - 31st, 2025

Advanced Quantum Algorithms & Future Applications

Explore advanced quantum algorithms, quantum optimization, and the future of quantum technologies.
